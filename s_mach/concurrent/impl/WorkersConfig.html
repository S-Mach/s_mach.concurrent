s_mach.concurrent.impl

WorkersConfig

trait WorkersConfig extends ProgressConfig with RetryConfig with ThrottleConfig

A trait for the configuration of a TraversableOnce.serially workflow that can wrap a concurrent function with progress reporting, retry and throttling functions

Note: Inheritance order here matters - throttle should be inner wrapper on f (progress and retry are interchangeable)
